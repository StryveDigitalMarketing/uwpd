Project Management Certificate

Consistently deliver projects on target, on time, and on budget

This in-class program provides you with the knowledge and skills to excel and succeed as a project manager. You will gain a solid understanding of sound project management techniques and processes, and the confidence to apply them effectively.

The curriculum is compatible with the project management industry standard set by the Project Management Institute (PMI). Waterloo partners with a PMI Registered Education Provider (REP) to provide you with PMP® Professional Development Units (PDUs).

Inquire

Courses

Complete the 2 compulsory courses (6 days) and a minimum of 4 days from the list of electives, for a total of at least 10 days and 70 PDUs.

Compulsory Courses

  • Project Management Applied Tools and Techniques (21 PDUs)
  • Advanced Project Monitoring and Control (21 PDUs)

Elective Courses (may be taken in any order)

  • People Skills: The Secret Behind Project Success (14 PDUs)
  • Juggling Multiple Projects (14 PDUs)
  • PMP® Exam Preparation (21 PDUs)
  • Business Analysis for the Project Manager (14 PDUs)
  • Microsoft Project 2010 or 2013 (14 PDUs)
